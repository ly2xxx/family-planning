## Key Risks & Considerations

### Technology Risks
1. **Quantum Winter**: Technology may take longer than expected to commercialize
2. **Competing Technologies**: Gate-based vs annealing vs topological - winners unclear
3. **Error Correction**: Fault-tolerant quantum computing still years away
4. **Scalability**: Qubit count increases may hit physical limits

### Market Risks
1. **Hype Cycles**: Quantum stocks experienced 2025 boom-bust (D-Wave: $5.77-$46.75 range)
2. **Dilution**: Cash-burning companies may issue more shares
3. **Acquisition Risk**: Small pure-plays may be acquired at unfavorable prices
4. **Competition**: Big tech can out-spend startups

### Execution Risks
1. **Product Delays**: Rigetti's Cepheus delay example
2. **Talent War**: Limited quantum scientists, high competition
3. **Customer Adoption**: Enterprise sales cycles long and uncertain

### Macro Risks
1. **Interest Rates**: High rates hurt unprofitable growth stocks
2. **Recession**: Enterprise IT budgets cut first
3. **Geopolitical**: U.S.-China tech competition, export controls
4. **Oil Prices**: Energy-intensive quantum systems affected by energy costs

**DO NOT** invest lump sum today. Use **Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)**:

**Year 1 (2026):**
- Month 1-3: 25% of planned investment
- Month 4-6: 25% of planned investment

- Month 10-12: 25% of planned investment

**Rationale:** Quantum stocks extremely volatile. D-Wave's 8x range in 52 weeks shows danger of single entry point.

### Portfolio Rebalancing
**Quarterly Review:**
- Rebalance if any position >40% or <5% (due to volatility)
- Take profits on >100% gainers, redeploy to laggards
- Cut losers down >70% from entry (preserve capital)

**Annual Strategic Review:**
- Reassess technology winners (gate-based vs annealing)
- Monitor commercialization progress
- Adjust pure-play vs mega-cap ratio based on market maturity

### Exit Strategy
**DO NOT** plan to hold individual pure-plays for full 10 years:

**3-5 Year Horizon for Pure-Plays:**
- IonQ, D-Wave, Rigetti likely acquired or face existential challenges
- Target: 3-5x return, then rotate to winners or mega-caps

**10-Year Horizon for Mega-Caps:**
- IBM, Alphabet, Microsoft can be held indefinitely
- Quantum becomes part of core business (like cloud)
