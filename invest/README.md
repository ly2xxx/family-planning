- ❌ Extreme volatility (D-Wave: $5.77-$46.75 in 52 weeks)

## 💡 Investment Philosophy

### Core Principles:

**1. Diversification is Critical**
- Never put all eggs in one quantum basket
- Combine pure-plays (high risk) with mega-caps (lower risk)
- Consider ETF for passive exposure

**2. Dollar-Cost Average (DCA)**
- Do NOT lump sum invest given volatility
- Spread purchases over 6-12 months
- Buy dips opportunistically

**3. Monitor Milestones, Not Daily Prices**
- 2026 IBM quantum advantage attempt = CRITICAL
- IonQ revenue growth trajectory = KEY
- Quarterly earnings > daily noise

**4. Long-Term Horizon Essential**
- Minimum 5 years, ideally 10 years
- Technology adoption takes time
- Early investors rewarded if patient

**5. Rebalance Discipline**
- Take profits when positions >40%
- Cut losses when down >70%
- Maintain target allocation
