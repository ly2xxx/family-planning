## Tax Considerations (UK Investor)

### Capital Gains Tax (CGT)
- **Allowance:** £3,000 tax-free (2026/27)
- **Rates:** 10% (basic) / 20% (higher rate)
- **Strategy:** Harvest losses in down years, realize gains in phases

### Dividend Tax
- **Allowance:** £500 tax-free (2026/27)
- **Rates:** 8.75% (basic) / 33.75% (higher) / 39.35% (additional)
- **Note:** IBM pays dividend (~4%), others don't

### ISA Wrapper
- ✅ Consider holding in Stocks & Shares ISA (£20,000 annual allowance)
- ✅ Tax-free growth and dividends
- ⚠️ Limited to UK-accessible platforms
- ⚠️ Check if U.S. stocks available (most ISAs allow)

### Stamp Duty
- ❌ No stamp duty on U.S.-listed stocks
- ✅ Avoids 0.5% UK stamp duty

## Emergency Action Plan

### If Quantum Winter Hits (Sector Down >50%)
1. **Reassess Thesis**: Is technology broken or just timing?
2. **Check Cash Positions**: Pure-plays must have >18mo runway
3. **Rotate if Needed**: Shift to mega-caps (IBM/GOOGL/MSFT)
4. **Dollar-Cost Average Down**: If fundamentals intact, buy dips
5. **Set Stop-Loss**: Protect remaining capital at -70% from entry

### If Quantum Boom Accelerates (Sector Up >200%)
1. **Take Profits**: Sell 25-50% of pure-play positions
2. **Rebalance**: Lock in gains, prevent over-concentration
3. **Raise Stops**: Protect profits with trailing stops
4. **Stay Disciplined**: Don't chase parabolic moves
5. **Prepare for Correction**: Quantum hype comes in cycles
