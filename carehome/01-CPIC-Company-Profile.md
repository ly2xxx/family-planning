## Recent Developments (2021-2026)

### **2021-2023: Healthcare Expansion**
- Established Guangci CPIC Internet Hospital in Shanghai
- Partnership with Huadong Hospital (华东医院) for two-way referral system
- Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) geriatrics specialty integration
- Shanghai Quality Control Center of Rehabilitation affiliation

### **2023-2024: Pacific Care Home Development**
- Shanghai facility scheduled opening: 2023
- Multiple locations in development across China
- Greater Bay Area (GBA) expansion announced
- Total floor space: 17,000+ square meters per facility

### **2024-2026: Service Enhancement**
- "Longevity Retreat" home-based care services
- CAR-T therapy coverage
- Proton-ion treatment services
- Immune-cell storage & therapy (Shanghai Cell Therapy Group partnership)
- Specialty drug coverage expansion
