## 📊 Financial Analysis: Insurance vs Bank Deposits

### The Core Question: Is It Better Than Bank Deposits?

**Bank Deposit Returns (2026):**
- 1年期: ~1.5-2.0% annual
- 3年期: ~2.5-3.0% annual
- 5年期: ~3.0-3.5% annual

**Insurance Pension Product Returns:**

#### Case Study: Taikang "幸福有约" Product
(RMB 2.4 million, 10-year payment)

| Year | Cash Value | Annual Payment (年金) | Total Return |
|------|-----------|---------------------|--------------|
| 第5年 | ~RMB 1.2M | RMB 63,000/年 | ~5% |
| 第10年 | ~RMB 2.6M | RMB 63,000/年 | ~8% |
| 第20年 | ~RMB 4.8M | RMB 63,/年 | ~15%+ |

**Key Points:**
- 年金 (annual payment): Typically 2.5-4% of total premium
- 现金价值 (cash value): Grows over time (typically 3-5% annual)
- 入住权 (residence right): Additional value

**Comparison:**

| Option | Annual Return | Risk Level | Care Backup |
|--------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| Bank Deposit | 1.5-3.5% | Very Low | ❌ None |
| Wealth Management | 3-5% | Medium | ❌ None |
| Insurance Pension | 3-6% + 入住权 | Low-Medium | ✅ Guaranteed |
| Direct Rental | - (pure cost) | N/A | ❌ Pay-as-you-go |

### Verdict: YES - Insurance Pension Communities Make Financial Sense

**If:**
1. You want better returns than bank deposits
2. You want a "底牌" (backup plan) for future care
3. You can afford the premium (RMB 1.5-2.4 million)
4. You plan to use the residence right eventually

**Why:**
- Returns: 3-6% annual (better than deposits)
- Risk: Lower than stocks/investments (insured by company)
- Value: 入住权 is valuable - healthcare costs rising
- Flexibility: Can gift to children, or not use and get cash value back
