## Step 4: Risk Assessment

### **Risk Tolerance Check:**

**For CPIC Pacific Care Home specifically:**

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| **Short operating history (1-3 years)** | 🔴 HIGH | Wait 2-3 years OR demand detailed performance data |
| **Limited public information** | 🟡 MEDIUM | Insist on facility tour, talk to current residents |
| **Smaller scale vs competitors** | 🟡 MEDIUM | Verify staff-to-resident ratio |
| **Unverified resident satisfaction** | 🔴 HIGH | Request references, check online reviews in 1-2 years |
| **CPIC financial strength** | 🟢 LOW | Moody's A3 rating, strong backing |
| **Medical insurance coverage** | 🟢 LOW | Huadong Hospital partnership confirmed |

**Risk Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Wait-and-see approach:** Monitor CPIC for 2-3 years, re-evaluate when track record established
2. **Short-term trial:** Negotiate 6-12 month trial period before full commitment
3. **Hybrid approach:** Move to established community now (Taikang, Qinheyuan), switch to CPIC later if proven

3. **Emergency access priority:** Unknown location distances → Choose **Vanke** or **Qinheyuan**
4. **Luxury seekers:** Not competitive vs **Taiping Wutong** or **Taikang**
5. **Risk-averse:** Short history = higher risk → Choose established operators
