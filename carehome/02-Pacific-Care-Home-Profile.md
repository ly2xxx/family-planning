# Pacific Care Home (太保家园) Profile

## Program Overview

**Brand Name:** Pacific Care Home (太保家园)  
**Operator:** China Pacific Life Insurance (CPIC Life)  
**Model:** CCRC (Continuing Care Retirement Community)  
**Launch:** 2021 (announced), 2023 (Shanghai opening)  
**Current Status:** Operational (Shanghai), expanding nationwide

## Strategic Positioning

CPIC's Pacific Care Home represents the company's entry into integrated **Insurance + Healthcare + Elderly Care** ecosystem, competing with:
- Taikang Home (泰康之家) - market leader
- China Taiping - Wutong Home (梧桐人家)
- China Life - Guoshou Jia (国寿嘉园)

## Three-Tier Service Model

According to CPIC official announcements, Pacific Care Home offers **three types of facilities** to cater to differentiated elderly needs:

### 1. **Active Living Communities (活力社区)**
- **Target:** Healthy, self-sufficient seniors (60-75 years)
- **Services:** Independent living units, social activities, wellness programs
- **Focus:** Quality of life, social engagement, preventive health

### 2. **Assisted Living (协助生活)**
- **Target:** Seniors needing some daily assistance (70-85 years)
- **Services:** Personal care, medication management, meals, housekeeping
- **Focus:** Maintaining independence with support

### 3. **Professional Nursing Care (专业护理)**
- **Target:** Seniors requiring full-time medical care
- **Services:** 24/7 nursing, rehabilitation, chronic disease management, memory care
- **Focus:** Medical-grade care, dignity, comfort

## Admission Requirements & Costs

### **Eligibility:**
- Typically 60+ years old
- Health assessment required
- Background check
- Financial verification

### **Cost Structure** (Estimated)
*Note: Pacific Care Home has not publicly disclosed detailed pricing. Based on comparable Shanghai CCRC facilities:*

**Entry Fee (会籍费/押金):**
- **Estimated Range:** RMB 200,000 - 800,000
- **Refundable:** Usually 70-90% refundable upon move-out
- **Variations:** Based on unit size and service level

**Monthly Service Fee (月费):**
- **Active Living:** RMB 5,000 - 12,000/month
- **Assisted Living:** RMB 10,000 - 20,000/month
- **Nursing Care:** RMB 15,000 - 30,000/month

**Additional Costs:**
- Meals: Usually separate (RMB 30-80 per meal)
- Medical services: Pay-per-use (insurance may cover)
- Activities: Mostly included, some premium events extra

### **Insurance Product Integration:**
- CPIC offers annuity products that can:
  - Waive or reduce entry fees
  - Provide monthly service fee coverage
  - Guarantee admission priority
- **Benefit:** Allows pre-planning and payment spreading
